Welcome to Al Shifaa

The unique location of the hospital makes it ideal for supporting clinical research that has relevance in uplifting the health of many hard to reach people living in informal urban settlements and poor rural communities. The focus is on establishing a clinical research centre/facility in collaboration with international partners, such as pharmaceutical organisations, academic institutions, charitable trusts etc.
Research activities will focus on:

  • Heart research – treatment and mechanistic studies
  • Tuberculosis and other infectious diseases
  • Community inherited and transmitted diseases
  • Traditional herbal medicines
